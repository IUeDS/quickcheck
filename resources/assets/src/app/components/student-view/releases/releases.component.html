Released quick check results

Your instructor chooses when to release a quick check so that results are visible to students. Results include all of your attempts at a quick check, count correct/incorrect, and score. Note that results are not the same as grades. Your grades will appear in the Canvas gradebook as soon as a quick check is graded, regardless of whether your instructor has chosen to release the results or not.
